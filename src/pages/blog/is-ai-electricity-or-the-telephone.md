<h1 id="-understanding-ai-electricity-and-the-telephone-"><strong>Understanding AI, Electricity, and the Telephone</strong></h1>
<p>Before diving into comparisons, let&#39;s briefly understand what AI, Electricity, and the Telephone are and their significance.</p>
<p>AI refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think, learn, and solve problems like humans. It has revolutionized industries such as healthcare, finance, and transportation, and it continues to hold tremendous potential for the future.</p>
<p>Electricity, on the other hand, is a fundamental form of energy that powers most modern technologies. It has enabled advancements across all sectors and drastically improved the quality of life for billions of people worldwide.</p>
<p>The Telephone, a revolutionary communication device invented by Alexander Graham Bell, laid the foundation for global interconnectedness and transformed the way we communicate with each other.</p>
